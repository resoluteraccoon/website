# MEET

We don't just study security — we **live it together**. This is where we meet, break things, and learn from each other in real time.

## What We Do

### Movie Nights
We dissect real breaches on screen. Equifax, SolarWinds, Target — we watch, pause, analyze, and debate what went wrong and how it could have been prevented.

### Hackathons
We compete. We build tools under pressure. We break CTF challenges. We fix what we break. Win or lose, we debrief as a team.

### Conferences & Webinars
Black Hat, DEF CON, BSides — we attend as a crew. We split up, cover more ground, and reconvene to share what we learned. We also run our own internal webinars.

### Newsletter
Weekly digest of new CVEs, tool releases, writeups worth reading, and crew announcements.

## Presentations

Every member presents. Whether it's a 5-minute lightning talk or a 30-minute deep dive, you will stand up and teach what you learned. Teaching is the final stage of learning.
