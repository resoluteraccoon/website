# Movie Saturdays

Every Saturday, we gather to dissect real breaches on screen. We watch, pause, analyze, and debate — what went wrong, how it could have been prevented, and what we learn for our own operations.

## Upcoming Screenings

|------|------|-------|

| TBD | *Citizenfour* | Snowden, NSA surveillance & encryption |

## Past Screenings

- **Equifax: The Breach** — How 147M records were exposed
- **The Social Dilemma** — Manipulation at scale
- **We Are Legion** — Anonymous & hacktivism

## How It Works

1. **Watch** — Full documentary, no interruptions
2. **Pause** — We stop at key moments to break down the technical details
3. **Debate** — Could it have been prevented? What would you have done differently?
