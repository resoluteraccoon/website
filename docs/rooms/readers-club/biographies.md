# Biographies

Lives of hackers, scientists, entrepreneurs, and visionaries. Not hero worship — case studies in how exceptional people think, decide, and persist.

**Hackers & Security Pioneers**  
*Ghost in the Wires* (Mitnick) — Social engineering as art. The human layer is always the weakest.  
*Kingpin* (Poulsen) — The transformation from black hat to journalist. The psychology of the underground.  
*Crypto* (Levy) — The cypherpunks, PGP, the crypto wars. How code became speech.  

**Scientists & Engineers**  
*Surely You're Joking, Mr. Feynman!* (Feynman) — Curiosity as a method. Play as research.  
*The Soul of a New Machine* (Kidder) — Building a minicomputer in 1980. The grind, the politics, the breakthrough.  
*Structures* (Petroski) — Why things fail. Engineering as learning from failure.  

**Founders & Builders**  
*Shoe Dog* (Knight) — Nike. Relentless iteration. Cash flow as oxygen.  
*The Wright Brothers* (McCullough) — Bicycle mechanics who solved flight. First principles, wind tunnels, self-funded.  
*Elon Musk* (Vance) — SpaceX, Tesla, the physics-first approach. First principles or bust.
