# Labour Market

The Labour Market is not about jobs. It is about **preparation**. It is the room where we confront the reality that technical skill is not enough — that a Cybersecuritist who cannot manage his health, his relationships, his finances, his discipline, is a Cybersecuritist who will break when the pressure comes.

We do not do vague motivational talk. We do **real conversations with accountability**. We look each other in the eye and say: *"You are over-talking."* Or: *"You are beginning to say nonsense."* We hold each other to a standard because we respect each other enough to be honest.

This room is where we practice **life as a system**. Health, discipline, marriage, business, finance, leadership, investing — each is a module. Each has protocols. Each is tracked, discussed, and improved.
