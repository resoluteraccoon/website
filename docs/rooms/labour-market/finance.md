# Finance

## Why This Matters

A Cybersecuritist who cannot manage money is a vulnerability waiting to be exploited. Finance is resource allocation under uncertainty — the same decision framework as exploit development, but the stakes are your freedom.

## What We Cover

- **Cash Flow Management** — Runway calculation. Burn rate monitoring. The same telemetry you use for infrastructure.
- **Investment as Compounding** — Time in market > timing the market. Index funds as diversified portfolio. Bitcoin as asymmetric hedge.
- **Tax Efficiency** — Legal optimization. The same attention to rules you apply to kernel exploits.
- **Risk Management** — Insurance. Emergency funds. Position sizing. Never bet the farm on a single trade.
- **Net Worth Tracking** — Monthly snapshots. Assets minus liabilities. The scoreboard you cannot ignore.

## The Standard

You know your numbers. Monthly. No surprises. Your financial position is as visible to you as your server uptime.
