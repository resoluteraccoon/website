# Marriage

## Why This Matters

Marriage is a long-term security operation. Two people building a shared perimeter. Trust is the encryption. Communication is the protocol. Shared vision is the mission.

## What We Cover

- **The Partnership Model** — Not 50/50. 100/100. Each person owns the whole. No "that's your job."
- **Communication Protocols** — Weekly syncs. Monthly reviews. Annual planning. The same rigor you apply to code reviews.
- **Conflict as Debugging** — Disagreements are not failures. They are edge cases revealing unhandled exceptions in your shared logic.
- **Financial Unity** — One budget. Shared goals. Transparent tracking. No hidden accounts, no hidden debt.
- **Family Vision** — What are you building together? Where are you in 5, 10, 20 years? The roadmap is explicit.

## The Standard

You do not hide vulnerabilities from your partner. You disclose them. You patch them together. The perimeter holds because both of you maintain it.
