# Business

## Why This Matters

A Cybersecuritist who cannot build and run a business is a mercenary, not an owner. Business is applied systems thinking — inputs, processes, outputs, feedback loops. The same principles that secure a network secure a company.

## What We Cover

- **Value Creation** — Identifying real problems. Building solutions people pay for. Product-market fit as vulnerability discovery.
- **Operations** — SOPs as playbooks. KPIs as telemetry. Automation as force multiplication.
- **Team Building** — Hiring as threat modeling. Culture as immune system. Delegation as privilege separation.
- **Sales & Marketing** — Reconnaissance. Value proposition as payload. Objection handling as bypass techniques.
- **Scaling** — Architecture decisions. Technical debt as strategic liability. Hiring faster than entropy.

## The Standard

You do not "try" business. You build a system that produces value. The metric is revenue, profit, and longevity — not vanity metrics.
