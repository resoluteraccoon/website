# Gentle Men's Health Session

## Why This Matters

A Cybersecuritist's body is his primary hardware. You cannot run exploits on a crashed kernel. You cannot maintain persistence with a corrupted filesystem. Physical health is not optional — it is infrastructure.

## What We Cover

- **Strength Protocols** — Compound movements. Progressive overload. Minimal effective dose. No gym-bro mythology.
- **Cardiovascular Capacity** — Zone 2 base building. VO2 max intervals. The engine that fuels deep work.
- **Mobility & Durability** — Joint health. Injury prevention. The prehab that prevents rehab.
- **Sleep Architecture** — Circadian alignment. Sleep hygiene as a security protocol. The recovery window.
- **Nutrition as Fuel** — Macronutrient timing. Micronutrient density. Eating for cognitive performance, not aesthetics.

## The Standard

You track. You measure. You adjust. No "I feel good." Data or it didn't happen. Your body is a system you are responsible for securing.
