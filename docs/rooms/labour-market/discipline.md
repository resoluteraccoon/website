# Discipline

## Why This Matters

Discipline is the gap between amateurs and professionals. Amateurs wait for motivation. Professionals build systems that execute regardless of feeling.

## What We Cover

- **The Definition** — Discipline is not willpower. It is environment design. It is reducing the friction of right action and increasing the friction of wrong action.
- **Daily Protocols** — Morning systems. Evening shutdowns. The non-negotiables that run on autopilot.
- **Deep Work Blocks** — Protecting cognitive bandwidth. The maker's schedule vs. the manager's schedule.
- **Accountability Systems** — Peer review. Public commitments. The cost of failure.
- **Recovery as Discipline** — Rest is not the absence of work. It is part of the work. Scheduled, protected, non-negotiable.

## The Standard

You do not negotiate with the protocol. The protocol exists because you decided it was right when you were thinking clearly. You execute when you are not.
