# Investing

## Why This Matters

Investing is deferred consumption deployed into productive assets. It is how a Cybersecuritist buys back their time. The goal is not to get rich quick — it is to never be forced into a bad decision by financial pressure.

## What We Cover

- **Asset Allocation** — The portfolio as a diversified system. Stocks, bonds, real estate, Bitcoin. No single point of failure.
- **Time in Market** — Compound interest is the most powerful exploit in finance. Start early. Stay invested. Ignore the noise.
- **Risk-Adjusted Returns** — Sharpe ratio. Sortino ratio. Maximum drawdown. The same metrics you use for detection tuning.
- **Behavioral Discipline** — Fear and greed are the adversary. Dollar-cost averaging removes emotion. Rebalancing enforces the plan.
- **Generational Wealth** — Trusts. Estate planning. Teaching the next cohort. The exploit that keeps paying.

## The Standard

You do not gamble. You allocate. You wait. You compound. The portfolio is a system you build once and let run — like a well-tuned detection rule.
