# Hackathons

Quarterly Hackathons: 48-hour operations against custom-built environments — web apps, thick clients, IoT firmware, cloud architectures, satellite comms. Teams of three. No internet. No AI assistants. Just you, your tools, and your teammates. Prizes aren't swag — they're mentorship, introductions, and real-world engagement invitations. The scoreboard is public. The lessons are permanent.
