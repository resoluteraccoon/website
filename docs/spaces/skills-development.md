# Skills Development

We do not just build hackers. We build **whole humans**.

Technical skill without supporting skills is a narrow blade — sharp but brittle. A Cybersecuritist who cannot cook, cannot film, cannot speak, cannot write — is a Cybersecuritist who cannot survive outside a terminal. We reject that limitation.

Skills Development is where we train the disciplines that make us **complete**:

- **[→ Cooking](/docs/spaces/skills-development/cooking)** — Precision, self-sufficiency, resource management.
- **[→ Videography](/docs/spaces/skills-development/videography)** — Documentation, narrative, presence.
- **[→ Public Speaking](/docs/spaces/skills-development/public-speaking)** — Communication under scrutiny, teaching under pressure.
- **[→ Deep Technical Communication](/docs/spaces/skills-development/deep-technical-communication)** — Translation without loss, leadership through clarity.

Each skill is not an elective. It is a **pillar**. Remove one, and the structure weakens.
