# Grow

Technical skill opens doors. Character keeps them open. Our spaces are designed to grow both.

## Our Spaces

### Readers Club
We read together — security papers, philosophy, fiction, history. Then we argue about it. Critical thinking isn't just for exploit development.

### Public Speaking
Weekly practice sessions. Impromptu talks. Prepared presentations. Feedback is honest and constructive. If you can explain a buffer overflow to a non-technical audience, you truly understand it.

### Real Life Conversations
Life outside the screen matters. We talk about career paths, burnout, ethics, and the human side of security. No performative tech-bro nonsense. Real talk.

### Men's Health
For the brothers who need space to be real. Mental health, physical fitness, relationships, purpose — we hold space for conversations that don't happen in Discord servers.

## Our Rule

**What happens here stays here.** No screenshots. No gossip. No using vulnerability against each other.

Technical skill and character growth. Both matter. We don't choose — we build both.
