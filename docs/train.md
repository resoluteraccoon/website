# TRAIN

We don't believe in scattered tutorials. We believe in **structured, locked learning paths** built from the best free platforms in the world.

## Our Platforms

### pwn.college
The gold standard for binary exploitation, reverse engineering, and systems security. We structure their content into progressive units with proof-of-completion gates.

### TryHackMe
Hands-on penetration testing labs. From beginner to advanced — we map their rooms into coherent learning tracks that build on each other.

### PortSwigger Web Security Academy
The definitive resource for web application security. We turn their labs into structured modules covering SQL injection, XSS, CSRF, authentication, and more.

## How It Works

1. **Pick a path** — Choose your focus area (Web, Binary, Network, etc.)
2. **Complete units** — Work through curated platform content
3. **Prove it** — Submit writeups, screenshots, or live demos
4. **Unlock next** — Advance only after demonstrated mastery

No skipping. No faking. Prove it or repeat it.
