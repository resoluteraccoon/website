# Community

Resolute Raccoon isn't a Discord server. It's a brotherhood of operators. No lurkers. No tourists. Every member has skin in the game — flags captured, code shipped, hours logged. The community is the force multiplier. You sharpen each other. You hold the line together. You celebrate the wins that matter.

## What We Do

### Readers Club
Deep reading for deep thinkers. Curated papers, post-mortems, exploit writeups, and seminal texts — discussed, dissected, and distilled in weekly sessions.

- **Format**: One paper per week. Everyone reads the full text before session. No summaries. No TL;DRs.
- **Scope**: From *Smashing the Stack for Fun and Profit* to Project Zero writeups, from Phrack archives to vendor advisories.
- **Output**: Annotated reading notes, discussion threads, and operator insights archived in our knowledge base.

### Public Speaking
Operators who can't communicate are liabilities. Public Speaking trains you to brief executives, teach peers, and defend findings under pressure.

- **Format**: Weekly 10-minute presentations, rotating topics. Live Q&A. No slides required — whiteboard only.
- **Topics**: Exploit chain walkthroughs, detection rule deep-dives, tooling demos, architecture defenses, post-mortem reviews.
- **Progression**: Start with 5-minute lightning talks. Advance to 30-minute technical briefings. Graduate to external conference talks.

### Real Life Conversations
The best ops happen off the record. Real Life Conversations are unscripted, off-the-record sessions with operators who've been in the trenches — nation-state defenders, red team leads, vulnerability researchers, founders who built security companies from zero.

- **Format**: Monthly 60-minute sessions. No recordings. No slides. Just operators talking to operators.
- **Guests**: Vetted practitioners with skin in the game. Names you'd recognize if you're in the field.
- **Rules**: Chatham House Rule. What's said here, stays here.

### Men's Health
The operator is the weapon. Men's Health covers sleep, nutrition, training, hormone optimization, stress physiology, and injury prevention — engineered for cognitive performance and physical resilience.

- **No bro-science**: Blood work. Biomarkers. Protocols.
- **Measurable**: Sleep quality (Oura/WHOOP), HRV, testosterone, cortisol, VO2 max.
- **Practical**: Training programs that fit around ops. Nutrition that fuels cognitive load. Recovery protocols that compound.
- **Community**: Accountability partners. Weekly check-ins. Quarterly blood panels.
